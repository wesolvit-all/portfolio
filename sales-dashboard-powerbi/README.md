# Sales Dashboard - Power BI

## Objective
Analyze sales performance through KPIs and interactive visualizations.

## Tools
- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query

## Metrics

* Net Revenue vs Previous Year
* Gross Margin
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Product Mix by Category
* Monthly Net Revenue vs Target
* Net Revenue by Sales Channel
* Monthly Target Achievement Rate
* Net Revenue by Sales Representative
* Quarterly Filters
